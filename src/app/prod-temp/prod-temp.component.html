Description

The human episomal iPSC reprogramming kit from ALSTEM is one of the best choices for producing footprint-free and virus-free iPSCs, which provide an ideal source for all stages of your pluripotent stem cell research. Optimized by the ALSTEM team, this kit has proven to be efficient and successful in inducing pluripotency from several different somatic cell types.
